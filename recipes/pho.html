Pho

Description

Pho is a soup consisting of bone broth, rice noodles, thinly-sliced meat (in this recipe, it's beef), and fresh garnishes.

Ingredients

  1. Beef Soup Bones: Use the shank and knee to make the homemade bone broth.
  2. Rice Noodles: You should be able to find dry rice noodles at most grocery stores, either in the international aisle or another pantry aisle.
  3. Beef: This recipe calls for beef top sirloin. Make sure it's thinly sliced so the hot broth can cook it.
  4. Fish Sauce: Fish sauce will give the pho a hint of salty, fishy flavor.
  5. Onion: Roast the onions for an extra depth of flavor.
  6. Spices and Seasonings: This recipe calls for ginger, star anise, and salt.
  7. Garnishes: Garnish your pho with cilantro, green onion, Thai basil, lime, hoisin sauce, sriracha, or anything else you like.
